Our Fake World

In this website, you will find my coding journey over a time period of 15 weeks. Everything has led up to the conception and creation of the final sketch which is a Fake World I would like to invite you to take a step into. The Fake World is an audio reactive and user interactive sketch, which you can use the Keys 1-4, R, F and basic trackpad functions to play around with.

Have a look at my process log, my p5js sketches, and github repo.
